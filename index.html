let's get right into it then


Welcome one and all

Hope you enjoy your visit. I'm creating this so I have a convenient place to write about things that interest me, or simply come across in day to day life - hence the name.

Sometimes it feels more appropriate to write about something rather than just bookmark it as a note-to-self that 'XYZ cool thing exists. Isn't that neat?'

Similarly, there are many times I'm working through a problem and find the process of scrawling train-of-consciousness notes therapeutic. Often I will go back and refine them down to pretty consise snippets, and it seems a shame not to share some of those daily successes and frustrations.

So hop on over to a link, if you like start right at the beginning.
